Siddhraj Z²

Modern Urban Plaza with Premium Office and Retail Spaces

Property Overview

The property is a modern marvel featuring a 100-foot-wide urban plaza with parking, seating areas, water bodies, and recreational zones. It offers seamless connectivity through a link road and a 30-foot-wide walkway on the second floor. With two levels of basement parking, 22 high-speed elevators, and airy, well-ventilated offices surrounding open courtyards, it ensures unmatched convenience. Highlights include a triple-height entrance foyer with Italian marble, a double-height recreational area, sitting decks on every floor, and a landscaped sky bridge on the fifth floor, making it a perfect blend of functionality and elegance.
