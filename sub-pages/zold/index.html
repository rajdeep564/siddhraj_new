Siddhraj Zold

Zold by Siddhraj Developers is a sustainable, luxury residential project blending eco-friendly design with modern comfort.

Property Overview

Zold by Siddhraj Developers offers a lifestyle of refined luxury with a unique blend of urban sophistication and countryside tranquillity. The contemporary design integrates a central green zone surrounded by landscaped amenities, including play areas, pools, and eco-conscious spaces. Interior specifications feature smooth plaster finishes, premium aluminium-glazed windows, masonite doors with wooden frames, designer porcelain tiles, and concealed copper wiring. Kitchens boast granite countertops with stylish tile detailing, while bathrooms include designer tile accents up to lintel height, ensuring every detail supports an elevated living experience.

Total Units:-140 (3BHK)
