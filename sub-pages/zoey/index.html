Property Overview

Zoey by Siddhraj Developers offers a modern lifestyle with a focus on blending luxury and comfort. This contemporary apartment complex features well-integrated green spaces, enhancing urban living with a touch of nature. Amenities include landscaped recreational areas, play zones, and eco-friendly facilities. High-end specifications provide smooth plastered walls, fully glazed aluminium windows, masonite doors, designer tile flooring, and concealed wiring. Kitchens and bathrooms are equipped with quality finishes, ensuring a sophisticated, sustainable, and comfortable living experience tailored to modern needs.
